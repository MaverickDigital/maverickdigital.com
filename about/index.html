About Us

Maverick Digital was created by two digital veterans to help companies develop, plan, exceute, measure and iterate in the digital era, after seeing a clear opportunity in the marketplace in how companies integrate digital technology into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how they operate and deliver value to customers. Belal and Rob came together to create Maverick Digital with the shared vision of helping companies integrate digital thinking and approaches into the core and DNA of their organizations. Many of the traditional approaches have failed to realise their original promise and business objectives. The path to growth isn’t always clear, and that’s why you need mavericks to lead the way.

Mavericks relentlessly strive towards our mission through our culture. Our values, the Maverick way, reflects that culture on a day-to day basis.

- Belal El-Harazin, Founder, CEO & Chief Maverick
